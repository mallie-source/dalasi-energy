About Dalati Energy

Who We Are

“We climb any mountain for our clients, because we care…”

Dalati (Pty) Ltd is a 100% Black-owned engineering company established in 2025. Dalati Energy was launched as a dedicated division focused on delivering innovative, reliable, and efficient energy solutions across Southern Africa.

Dalati Energy is an authorized associate of Powerlux, a brand of Powerlux Industries Pvt Ltd. Through this partnership, we supply affordable silent diesel generators and portable tower lights catering to the industrial, commercial, construction, and residential sectors.

With a leadership team bringing over 20 years of combined experience across manufacturing, construction, mining, and engineering services, Dalati Energy is actively bidding on strategic projects with state-owned enterprises and municipalities across South Africa.
